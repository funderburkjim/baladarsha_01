How to Use Sanskrit Flashcards

  • Click the flashcard to flip and reveal the English meaning.
  • Use “Next” and “Prev” to navigate through the shuffled list.
  • Use “Choose Vocabulary” to load a new set from the dropdown.
  • The “Info” field may show gender, verb class, or other metadata.
  • All data is loaded locally—no files are uploaded to a server.

You can revise this page to include grammar tips, Sanskrit conventions, or links to external resources.
